Teaching Philosophy

My Approach to Teaching

I believe economics education should connect theoretical concepts to real-world applications. My teaching emphasizes critical thinking, data literacy, and the ability to analyze economic problems from multiple perspectives. I strive to create an inclusive classroom environment where all students feel comfortable engaging with challenging material and asking questions.
